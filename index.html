Spring Fair is an exciting time for Albany Preschool. It's the biggest bash that our students (and our co-op parents) throw every year — enjoying seasonal fun and games, whilst also aiming to raise as much funding as possible to help keep our beloved preschool running. APS has been a community staple for local kids since it first opened back in 1937.

This will be the first Spring Fair since 2019, and we aim to make it a good one! There will be staple activities like the APS raffle, a box maze and plethora of baked goods for all to enjoy. Additionally, we will host our inaugural APS Bike-A-Thon event for cyclists of all ages.

Sponsored by Xtracycle, who are providing prizes.

Registration

The Route

The route is a simple triangle utilizing the Ohlone Greenway, to ensure a simple and safe journey for riders of all ages.

Participants will have 30 mins to ride laps of the route, which will be tallied and scores used to solicit donations for APS.

Fundraising

The goal is for participants to collect sponsors, who will donate per lap cycled! This will be handled through a Give Butter campaign page, which will be added soon.

Alternatively, a $20 registration donation can be offered for those who would rather cycle at a more casual party pace!

Contact development@albanypreschool.org if you have any questions regarding this event.
