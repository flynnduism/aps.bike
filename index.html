Spring Fair is an exciting time for Albany Preschool. It's the biggest bash that our little students (and our co-op parents) throw every year — enjoying seasonal fun and games, whilst also aiming to raise as many funds as possible for our beloved preschool, which has been a community staple for local kids since it opened its' doors back in 1937!.

This will be the first Spring Fair since 2019, and we aim to make it a good one! There will be staple activities like the APS raffle, a box maze and plethora of baked goods for all to enjoy. Additionally, we will host our inaugural APS Bike-A-Thon event for cyclists of all ages.
